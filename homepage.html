Java edition

The original version of Minecraft. Notch began development on May 10, 2009, publicly releasing Minecraft on May 17, 2009. The full release of the game (version 1.0.0) was on November 18, 2011, at MINECON 2011. Java edition is also the house of moded minecraft

Bedrock edition

Minecraft: Bedrock Edition (officially just Minecraft) is the multi-platform versions of Minecraft developed by Mojang Studios, Xbox Game Studios, and SkyBox Labs and based on the Bedrock codebase. Cross play with friends on PC, Xbox, Playstation and even on your Andriod or Apple devices

Minecraft Legends

Minecraft Legends is an upcoming real-time action strategy video game being developed by Mojang Studios, Xbox Game Studios, and Blackbird Interactive, which would be released in 2023. Team up with all overworld mobs (even hostile ones) and stop the invasion of the piglin army!!!

Minecraft Dungeons

Minecraft's action-adventure RPG. Brave the dungeons alone, or team up with friends! Fight with up to 4 player, explore new uncertain land, gain loot and take down the Arch-Illager!!! Get exclusive prizes with the each season pass!

Minecraft Earth

Minecraft's very own augmented reality game for your mobile device, that lets you bring Minecraft into the real world! Go on exciting adventure and create incredible builds with friends. Bask in your life sized creation, every Minecrafter's dream!!

Education Edition

Minecraft's Education Edition offers a variety of academically-focused learning plans, ranging from fractions to fairytales, to inspire student-centered, interactive exploration and building. Examples: learn math with the Oregon Trail Experience, go on an adventure across the rugged landscape of North America; solve activities across STEM, Humanities, and even Fine Arts. Think through the ratios of supplies needed, strategize on crossing rivers.
